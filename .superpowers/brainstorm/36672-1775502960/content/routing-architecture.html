A — Router-first pipeline

Router annotates every step before execution. Each step knows its model upfront. Escalation is a step type that re-routes. Clean, predictable, easy to log and dashboard.

B — Signal-driven reactive

Everything defaults to Codex+bash (free). A signal bus watches for failure patterns, keywords, and time. LLMs are invoked reactively when signals fire. Most jobs never touch Sonnet.
